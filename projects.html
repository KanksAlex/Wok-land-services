Villa Sri Kampung

location: PT188, Villa Sri Kampung, Kampung Syed Nahar, Kuah, 07000 Kuah, Malaysia – Categories: - Investment, major Shareholder

Featuring river views, Villa Sri Kampung provides accommodations with a balcony, around 3.4 km from Langkawi Bird Paradise. Located 3.4 km from Sungai Kilim Nature Park, the property has a garden and free private parking. The air-conditioned villa consists of 1 separate bedroom, 1 bathroom with a hairdryer and free toiletries, and a seating area. The kitchen comes with a microwave, a fridge and a stovetop, as well as an electric tea pot. The villa offers a continental or halal breakfast. A car rental service is available at Villa Sri Kampung. Langkawi Kristal is 3.5 km from the accommodation, while Dataran Helang is 5.5 km from the property. The nearest airport is Langkawi, 17.7 km from Villa Sri Kampung, and the property offers a free airport shuttle service. Couples in particular like the location – they rated it 8.9 for a two-person trip. We speak your language!
